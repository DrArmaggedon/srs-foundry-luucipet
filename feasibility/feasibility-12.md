### MARGINAL detail — SRS-ENV-0012 (Enclosure UV Stabilization)
- **Why not FAIL:** D3's coarse-screen authority only fails a requirement when NO conceivable verification path exists. VM:Analysis (checking UV-stabiliser formulation/datasheet claims against the "UV-stabilized" outcome) is a conceivable path, so the bar for FAIL is not met.
- **Why MARGINAL:** The requirement lacks the exposure-duration/test-method quantification its sibling ENV-0013 has (2,000 h / IEC 60068-2-5), and this gap is explicitly PRD-absent (no invented number was substituted) — correctly handled per the section's established convention, but it does leave the declared VM:Analysis weaker/less rigorous than a bounded test.
- **Routing:** Per the authority boundary, whether VM:Analysis is the *appropriate* method for this requirement (vs. requiring a bounded exposure test) is the Verification-Method Validator's call, not this review's. Flagging forward per dispatch instructions ("flag if needed").
- **Pre-existing UNRESOLVED-CONTEXT flags:** This MARGINAL corresponds to Drafter-flag #1 (enclosure UV-exposure test duration/method absent from PRD) already logged in §12's Drafter Notes — no new gap discovered, confirming the Drafter's own flag.

### Advisory notes (non-blocking)
- **SHOULD-strength requirements (ENV-0004, ENV-0010, ENV-0011):** Correctly reflect PRD §13.4's "recommended" (non-mandatory) framing for damp-heat, shock, and vibration testing. Not a feasibility defect — normative strength (shall vs. should) is a Drafter/PCP convention matter, consistent with priority (MEDIUM) assigned to all three.
- **Derived retention requirements (ENV-0017, ENV-0018):** Both PRD-silent but soundly and symmetrically derived from the explicitly-stated thermal-cycling case (ENV-0015) acting on the identical safety-critical mechanism (Zone 2 fuse tab, SAFE-0001/2/3 windows). Assessed as sound derivations, not scope creep — flagged here only for visibility, consistent with the Drafter's own UNRESOLVED-CONTEXT flag #2.
- **Regulatory basis (D2, all 18 reqs):** All cited IEC 60068-2-x/-60529 standards (used across ENV-0001–0018) fall under RM-0028, CONFIRMED (clause INDICATIVE) — no CONFIRMED/INDICATIVE Regulatory Map entry is contradicted by any §12 requirement. RM-0024/RM-0031 (GPSR breakaway safety case) are reinforced, not conflicted, by ENV-0015/0017/0018's fuse-force retention criteria.
- **Cross-reference integrity (D4, all 18 reqs):** Every XR target cited in §12 (SRS-HW-0001/0002/0003/0006/0007/0010, SRS-SAFE-0001/0002/0003, SRS-INT-0044, and intra-section SRS-ENV-* refs) was confirmed present in its owning section (§7/§10/§11) — no dangling cross-references.
- **Out-of-scope items (Base Station, Travel Charging Cradle environmental durability) and the CR-driven vocabulary-convention note** are Drafter-logged scope/format flags, not feasibility defects — no action needed from this review.
